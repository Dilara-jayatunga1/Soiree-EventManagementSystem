FAQs

Can I bring my own decorations?
Yes, you're welcome to bring your own decorations. However, please let us know in advance about any specific requirements or restrictions.
Is outside catering allowed?
Unfortunately, we do not allow outside catering. Our team will provide a variety of catering options to suit your needs.
Do you provide entertainment services?
Yes, we offer a range of entertainment services including DJs, live bands, and performers. Please contact us for more information and pricing.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of event and the services booked. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact our team for more information.
Can I bring my own alcohol?
No, outside alcohol is not allowed. We have a fully stocked bar with a variety of beverages available for purchase.
Is parking available?
Yes, we have ample parking space available for guests.
